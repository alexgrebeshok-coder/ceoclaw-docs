# From MBA Thesis to AI Product: The CEOClaw Story

*Published: April 2026*

---

## The Spark

In 2024, I was tasked with implementing a Corporate Project Management System (КСУП) at Severavtodor, a road construction company in Russia's Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug. It's the kind of place where temperatures drop to -50°C, construction seasons last 2-3 months, and supply chains depend on winter roads that exist only when rivers freeze.

After 24 years in construction project management — from engineer to Director of Capital Construction for the city of Surgut — I knew the problem intimately. The tools just didn't fit.

## The MBA Approach

I enrolled in the MBA program at RANEPA (Russian Presidential Academy of National Economy), specializing in Project Management. My thesis wasn't just academic — it was a working prototype:

- **ROI analysis:** 171% over 3 years, with 4-5 month payback
- **Savings model:** ₽120M annually per company
- **Methodology:** 580 pages of PM documentation
- **Validation:** 1,700+ automated test scenarios

The core insight: **AI can augment construction PMs, not replace them.** An AI-powered PMO could automate reporting, predict problems before they escalate, and preserve institutional knowledge.

## Building with AI

The thesis was the blueprint. But building it required a new approach.

Over 6 months, I used AI coding agents — primarily Claude Code and GitHub Copilot — to write 90%+ of the codebase. The AI agents handled implementation while I provided domain expertise, architecture decisions, and quality control.

**Result:** CEOClaw v0.1.0 — a full-featured AI-powered PM platform with:
- 73 database models
- 208 API routes
- 5 specialized AI agents
- Multi-provider AI (OpenRouter, Zhipu AI, OpenAI)
- Multi-language support (Russian, English, Chinese)

## What Makes It Different

1. **Built by a practitioner** — not a tech company guessing what construction needs
2. **AI-native architecture** — agents aren't bolted on; they're the foundation
3. **1C integration** — works with Russia's dominant enterprise software
4. **Arctic-ready** — offline mode, GPS/GLONASS, designed for harsh environments
5. **Free for PMs** — because AI tools should be accessible to everyone

## What's Next

CEOClaw is currently in beta. We're looking for:
- **Beta users** — construction companies willing to pilot the platform
- **Partners** — 1C integrators and construction consultancies
- **Feedback** — from anyone who manages construction projects

The construction industry doesn't need another generic PM tool. It needs an AI-powered platform that understands roads, winter, concrete, and the people who build them.

That's what CEOClaw is.
