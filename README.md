## The Problem

Construction projects are failing — not because of bad engineering, but because of broken management:

- 📊 **30% of construction budget** is lost to poor coordination (McKinsey)
- 📱 **5+ disconnected tools** per project — WhatsApp, Excel, email, paper forms
- ⏱️ **73% of projects** exceed their planned schedule (Russian construction industry)
- 🧊 **Zero specialized tools** for Arctic and remote-site construction

Project managers spend more time **reporting** than actually **managing**.

## The Solution

**CEOClaw** is a single AI-powered platform that replaces the chaos:

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| 🤖 **5 AI Agents** | PMO Director, Planning, Monitoring, Financial Control, Knowledge |
| 📈 **EVM Analytics** | Earned Value Management — CPI, SPI, EAC in real-time |
| 📊 **Live Dashboard** | Portfolio overview with AI insights |
| 🗓️ **Smart Scheduling** | AI-powered Gantt charts with delay prediction |
| 🔗 **1C Integration** | Pull data from 1C:ERP/PM — no double entry |
| 📋 **Automated Reports** | Weekly/monthly status in one click |
| 🗺️ **Multi-language** | Russian, English, Chinese |

## Origin Story

<details>
<summary><strong>📖 From MBA Thesis to AI Product in 6 Months</strong></summary>

**Alexander Grebeshok** spent 24 years managing construction projects in Russia's harshest environments — from Surgut to the Yamal Peninsula, where temperatures drop to -50°C and supply chains stretch across frozen tundra.

In 2024, as Advisor to the CEO at **Severavtodor** (Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug), he was tasked with implementing a Corporate Project Management System (КСУП). He discovered what every construction PM knows: **the tools don't fit the job**.

So he went back to school. An MBA at **RANEPA** (Russian Presidential Academy) gave him the framework to think about the problem systematically. His thesis became the blueprint for an AI-powered PMO — with real financials: **ROI 171%, payback in 4-5 months, ₽120M annual savings**.

**6 months later**, working primarily with AI coding tools, he built **CEOClaw v0.1.0** — the construction PM platform he wished he'd had for two decades.

> *"I didn't start a startup because I wanted to be a founder. I started it because after 24 years of fighting broken processes, I finally had the tools to fix them."*

- **2001–2024:** 24 years in construction PM (Surgut, Yamal, KhMAO)
- **2024:** MBA, RANEPA — thesis on AI in construction PM
- **2024–2025:** КСУП implementation at Severavtodor (7+ concurrent projects)
- **2025–2026:** Built CEOClaw v0.1.0 (solo founder + AI agents)
