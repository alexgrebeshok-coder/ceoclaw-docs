# Executive Brief

## CEOClaw — AI-Powered PM for Construction

### One-Sentence Summary

CEOClaw is an AI-powered project management platform purpose-built for construction companies — turning chaos into operational intelligence with **171% projected ROI**.

### The Problem

| Statistic | Source |
|-----------|--------|
| 30% of construction budget lost to poor coordination | McKinsey |
| 73% of projects exceed schedule | Russian construction industry |
| 40% of PM time spent on reporting | Industry average |
| Zero specialized PM tools for Arctic construction | Market gap |

### The Solution

Single AI platform replacing 5+ disconnected tools:

- **5 AI Agents** — PMO Director, Planning, Monitoring, Financial, Knowledge
- **Real-time EVM** — CPI, SPI, EAC from 1C data automatically
- **Predictive Analytics** — AI detects delays and overruns before they happen
- **1C Integration** — pull data from Russia's dominant enterprise software
- **Free for PMs** — AI tools as a right, not a luxury
