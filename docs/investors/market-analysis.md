- Too small for enterprise solutions (Procore, PlanRadar)
- Active AI adoption interest
- Budget for SaaS tools

## Market Trends

### Favorable

1. **AI adoption acceleration** — Russian companies increasingly open to AI tools
2. **1C ecosystem** — 1C is dominant; integration is a must-have differentiator
3. **Government digitalization** — mandatory ФГИС ЦС pushes companies toward digital PM
4. **Talent shortage** — experienced PMs are scarce; AI augments their capabilities
5. **Import substitution** — Western tools (Procore, PlanRadar) face barriers; local alternatives needed

### Challenging

1. **Conservative industry** — construction is slow to adopt new technology
2. **Price sensitivity** — many companies accustomed to "free" (Excel, WhatsApp)
3. **Fragmented market** — thousands of small companies, hard to reach
4. **Infrastructure** — remote sites have poor connectivity
5. **Regulation** — complex compliance requirements vary by region
