# Origin Story: From MBA Thesis to AI Product

*By Alexander Grebeshok*

---

I spent 24 years managing construction projects in some of the most challenging environments on Earth — Surgut, Khanty-Mansiysk, and the Yamal Peninsula in Russia's Arctic. Where temperatures drop to -50°C, supply chains stretch across frozen tundra, and construction windows shrink to 2-3 months per year.

In 2024, I became Advisor to the CEO at **Severavtodor**, a road construction company in the Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug (YNAO). My task: implement a Corporate Project Management System (КСУП) across 7+ concurrent projects.

## The Problem I Knew Too Well

Every construction PM knows this pain:

- **5+ disconnected tools** — WhatsApp for communication, Excel for budgets, email for approvals, paper forms for site documentation
- **Late problem detection** — budget overruns discovered 2 months too late
- **Reporting trap** — project managers spend 40% of their time writing reports instead of managing
- **Knowledge loss** — when a PM leaves, their experience leaves with them

The existing PM tools didn't fit. Jira is built for software. Monday.com is too generic. Procore is Western, expensive, and doesn't understand Russian construction regulations (SNiP, GOST).

## Back to School

I decided to approach the problem systematically. I enrolled in the **MBA program at RANEPA** (Russian Presidential Academy of National Economy and Public Administration), specializing in Project Management.

My MBA thesis became more than an academic exercise — it became a **working blueprint** for solving real construction PM problems with AI:

- **ROI: 171%** over 3 years
- **Payback period: 4-5 months**
- **Annual savings: ₽120M** per company
- **580 pages** of PM methodology documentation
- **1,700+** automated test scenarios

The thesis proved that an AI-powered PMO could transform construction project management — not by replacing PMs, but by giving them superpowers.

## Building CEOClaw

Armed with the thesis framework and 24 years of field experience, I started building. But I had a secret weapon: **AI coding agents**.

Using Claude Code, GitHub Copilot, and custom AI tools, I built CEOClaw v0.1.0 in **6 months** — as a solo founder. The AI agents wrote 90%+ of the code while I provided domain expertise, architecture decisions, and quality control.

**CEOClaw** (CEO + Claw, like OpenClaw) is the platform I wished I'd had for two decades:

- **5 specialized AI agents** — PMO Director, Planning, Monitoring, Financial Control, Knowledge Management
- **Real-time EVM analytics** — CPI, SPI, EAC calculated automatically from 1C data
- **Predictive capabilities** — AI detects delays and cost overruns before they happen
- **1C integration** — pulls data from Russia's dominant enterprise software
- **Built for harsh environments** — offline mode, GPS/GLONASS, photo documentation

## The Philosophy

> *"I didn't start a startup because I wanted to be a founder. I started it because after 24 years of fighting broken processes, I finally had the tools to fix them."*

CEOClaw is built on three principles:

1. **Domain-first** — Built by a construction PM, not a Silicon Valley startup guessing what you need
2. **AI-augmented** — AI makes PMs more effective, doesn't replace them
3. **Accessible** — Free forever for individual project managers. AI tools should be a right, not a luxury.
