## Why Free?

1. **Market adoption** — Construction PM tools are expensive and rare in Russia. Free tier drives adoption.
2. **Data network effects** — More users = better AI models = better predictions for everyone.
3. **Founder's mission** — Alexander Grebeshok spent 24 years with inadequate tools. He wants to fix that.
4. **Freemium proven** — Slack, Notion, Figma all grew through free tiers. We follow the same playbook.

## Revenue Model

- **Free tier:** Adoption, community, word-of-mouth
- **Pro subscriptions:** Recurring revenue from growing teams
- **Enterprise contracts:** Custom deals with large construction companies
- **Implementation services** (future): Setup, training, 1C integration consulting
- **Marketplace** (future): Third-party plugins and templates
