# Partner Economics

## Revenue Share Model

### Implementation Partner

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| CEOClaw license (per customer) | ₽50K/month (Pro) |
| Partner share | 20-30% of first-year revenue |
| Average deal size | ₽600K/year |
| **Partner revenue per deal** | ₽120-180K |
| Implementation fee (partner keeps) | ₽200-500K |

### Example: 1C Franchisee Partner

| Scenario | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Customers per year | 10 |
| Implementation fee (partner keeps) | ₽300K × 10 = ₽3M |
| Revenue share (25%) | ₽150K × 10 = ₽1.5M |
| **Total partner annual revenue** | **₽4.5M** |

## Value Proposition for Partners

1. **New revenue stream** — AI-PMO is a growing market with no established players
2. **Customer retention** — CEOClaw makes your 1C implementations more valuable
3. **Differentiation** — offer AI-powered PM as add-on to 1C deployments
4. **Low effort** — CEOClaw is SaaS; no complex on-premise installation
5. **Growing market** — Russian construction digitalization is accelerating
